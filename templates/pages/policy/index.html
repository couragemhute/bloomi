Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Effective Date: {{ today|default:"January 1, 2024" }}

MistNex Dynamics (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is a tech company offering digital communication tools such as WhatsApp messaging platforms, chatbots, and automation services. We are committed to safeguarding your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact data (name, email address, phone number)
  • Message content (WhatsApp or contact forms)
  • Technical data (IP address, device type, browser info)
  • Business profile data (company name, industry, preferences)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and manage our WhatsApp messaging solutions
  • To communicate with you about services, billing, or issues
  • To analyze user behavior and improve our platform
  • To comply with Meta's and regulatory requirements

3. WhatsApp Messaging Policy

  • We process messages and metadata strictly for service delivery
  • We do not read or store personal conversations without explicit permission
  • We use secure channels and APIs approved by WhatsApp/Meta

4. Data Sharing with Trusted Providers

  • We do not sell or lease your data to third parties
  • We may share limited data with:
    • Meta (Facebook/WhatsApp)
    • Twilio or other communication platforms
    • Hosting providers and analytics tools
  • All partners follow strict data privacy terms

5. Data Retention

  • Your data is retained only as long as needed for service purposes
  • Chat logs and metadata are auto-purged where possible
  • You may request deletion at any time

6. Cookies & Analytics

  • We use cookies to improve site functionality and security
  • Analytics cookies help us understand visitor behavior
  • You can disable cookies in your browser settings

7. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we store about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Contact your local data authority if your rights are violated

8. Data Security

  • We use HTTPS encryption and secure databases
  • Only authorized staff can access your data
  • We regularly update and audit our systems

9. Children's Privacy

  • Our services are not meant for children under 13
  • We do not knowingly collect data from minors
